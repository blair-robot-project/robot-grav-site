## Become a... 
### Sponsor

Help support Team 449 as we learn! [Read about](https://robot.mbhs.edu/sponsor-information/sponsor-benefits) our sponsor benefits and sponsorship levels, then [reach out](mailto:blair.robot@gmail.com) about a partnership.

### Member

Come join us! Our team welcomes all high-school students, regardless of school or program. We meet at Montgomery Blair High School in Silver Spring, Maryland. Come up to Room 361 on Thursdays from 3:30 to 6:30 to meet the team and get involved. Questions?  [Just ask](mailto:blair.robot@gmail.com)!

### Mentor

 [Tell us](mailto:blair.robot@gmail.com) about yourself! Depending on your area of expertise, we'll pair you with a subteam. We'd love to work with you!

**We'd love to hear from you! Email us at [blair.robot@gmail.com](mailto:blair.robot@gmail.com)!**
