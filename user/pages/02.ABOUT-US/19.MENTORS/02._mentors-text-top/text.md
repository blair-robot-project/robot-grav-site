In 2013, **449 mentor Sharon Goodall received the [Woodie Flowers Finalist Award](https://www.woodieflowers.org/)** at the [Pittsburgh Regional](https://www.thebluealliance.com/event/2013papi#awards). The award honors a mentor who best leads, inspires, and empowers students through excellent communication — and who embodies the values of FIRST. It's one of the most respected individual honors that FIRST bestows, and it remains a point of pride for the team.
