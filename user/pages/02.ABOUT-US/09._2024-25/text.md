### 2024-25

Team 449, along with their alliance partners, won:
* **[2025 _FIRST_ Chesapeake District Championship](https://frc-events.firstinspires.org/2025/team/449)** 
* **[2025 CHS District Severn MD Event](https://www.thebluealliance.com/event/2025mdsev#results)**
* **[2025 CHS District Glen Allen VA Event](https://www.thebluealliance.com/event/2025vagle)** 

At Severn, 449 also received the event's highest award, the **[_FIRST_ Impact award](https://www.firstinspires.org/resources/library/frc/fia-resources)**. The [award citation](https://frc-events.firstinspires.org/2025/MDSEV/awards) read: "This highly motivated team created meaningful and lasting changes within their community. Their extensive mentorship efforts have this team breaking down barriers and extending access to stem education. Their dedication to inclusion, innovation, & outreach shows with their annual BUNNYBOTS competition."
