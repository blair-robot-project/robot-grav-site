##Tumbleweed
Our robot for the 2026 First Robotics Competition, REBUILT. Tumbleweed features an horizontally extending hopper, a double shooter, and can drive under the trench.

#### Performance
* 49-15-0 (W-L-T) in official play
* At the _FIRST_ Chesapeake District Championship, the team went 17-0-0—undefeated—to win for the second straight year.
