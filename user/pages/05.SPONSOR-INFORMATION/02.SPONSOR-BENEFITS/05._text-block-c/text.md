### Our impact, by the numbers
In the past three years, Team 449:
* Gave **2,100+ volunteer hours** at 32 community events.
* Reached **4,000+ people**—students, families, and community members.
* Drew **18 teams to 2025 [Blair Bunnybots](/bunnybots)**, an all-time high for the off-season FRC event we design and host.
* Welcomed **49 FIRST LEGO League teams** to the two Montgomery County qualifying tournaments we hosted
* Grew from **70 to 100 students**. Nearly one-third of the team is female.
* **Founded FLL Team 57535; mentored FTC Team 27025**, which reached the Chesapeake District Championship for the first time this year.

_Source: 2026 Team 449 Impact Statement_
