### **Sponsorship levels**

**Your name and logo will travel** with one of the region's most visible robotics programs. We can display it on our robot and team apparel at multi-day district competitions, on our Bunnybots livestream, and across the schools and community events we reach throughout the year.

<a id="bronze"></a>
#### Bronze — $449
* Name and logo on our sponsor page
* Name on team T-shirt, brochure, and banner

<a id="silver"></a>
#### Silver — $1,000 · includes Bronze, plus:
* Name on our robot
* Logo on team T-shirt, brochure, and banner

<a id="gold"></a>
#### Gold — $2,500 · includes Silver, plus:
* Logo on our robot
* Name and logo on our homepage
* Listed with Team 449 on official FIRST websites
<a id="diamond"></a>

#### Diamond — $5,000 · includes Gold, plus:
* **A Team 449 robot demo at your workplace or event**
* Name announced with Team 449 at competitions

<a id="title"></a>
#### Title Sponsor — $6,000 · includes Diamond, plus:
* Title sponsorship of [Blair Bunnybots](/bunnybots) or our _FIRST_ LEGO League tournament
* Limited to one per event

See a level that fits? Want to design something custom? **We're happy to build one around your goals.** Team 449 welcomes corporate and foundation sponsorships, as well as in-kind donations, technical services, equipment, materials, mentor support, and other forms of partnership. Email us at [blair.robot@gmail.com](mailto:blair.robot@gmail.com?subject=Interested%20%in%20%Sponsorship)!

### Donations and tax information

Team 449 accepts donations through the [Montgomery Blair High School Magnet Foundation, Inc.](https://www.mbhsmagnet.org/welcome-to-the-magnet-foundation), our fiscal sponsor. The Foundation is a Maryland public charity exempt from federal income tax under section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Contributions to the Foundation are deductible under section 170 of the Code. The Foundation’s Employer Identification Number is 26-2790557.

To donate online, use this [PayPal](https://www.paypal.com/donate/?cmd=_s-xclick&hosted_button_id=WPMD8G4P572UJ) link.
