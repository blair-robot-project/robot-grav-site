## Why back us?

Sponsoring Team 449 is a **direct investment in STEM and in the community**: in high-school students who are learning design, engineering, software, fabrication, teamwork, and leadership — and in the younger students and families we mentor and inspire through outreach.

Robotics here is **real engineering under pressure**. Students design, build, program, and field complex machines on hard deadlines. That rigor shows up in competition: Team 449 has won back-to-back _FIRST_ Chesapeake District Champions, and is one of just five FRC teams ever to do so with an undefeated run. 

Those skills carry forward: 100% of recent Team 449 graduates went on to higher education, with **92% of them pursuing STEM fields.** And they ripple out. Over the past three years, our students have given 2,100+ volunteer hours at 32 community events. We **reached thousands of K–12 students** across Montgomery County, including by running STEM Fairs at Title I elementary schools to bring hands-on robotics to kids who might never otherwise see it.

**We stay open to everyone:** no dues, no roster caps, no experience required, with travel and event costs waived for members who need it. Your sponsorship develops tomorrow's engineers and widens the door behind them.
